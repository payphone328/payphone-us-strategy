Market Intelligence

US Domestic Migration Patterns (2025-2026)

Understanding where immigrant communities are moving within the US helps prioritize merchant acquisition efforts. Follow the migration, capture the merchant opportunity.

Immigrant Migration Flows Within USA

As of 2025-2026, strong internal migration patterns show Hispanic/Latino populations moving from traditional gateway states (CA, NY, NJ) to Sun Belt states (FL, TX, NC, SC, GA, AZ) driven by affordability, jobs, and climate.

California → Texas, Arizona, Nevada

California has highest outbound migration in US. Hispanic families moving to TX (Austin, Houston, Dallas), AZ (Phoenix), NV (Las Vegas) for lower cost of living.

🔻 CA → 🔺 TX/AZ/NV
  • Merchant Opportunity: Transplanted CA merchants need new payment processors
  • Strategy: Target CA expats in Austin, Phoenix, Vegas with "CA merchant discount"

New York/New Jersey → Florida, North Carolina

NY/NJ seeing massive exodus to FL (Miami, Tampa, Orlando) and NC (Charlotte, Raleigh). Tax advantages and remote work driving moves.

🔻 NY/NJ → 🔺 FL/NC
  • Merchant Opportunity: Ecuadorian/Colombian merchants from NY/NJ to FL
  • Strategy: Payphone Ecuador brand recognition = trust in FL market

Illinois → Florida, Texas, North Carolina

Illinois (esp. Chicago) losing population to FL, TX, NC. Mexican-American community relocating for warmer climate and lower taxes.

🔻 IL → 🔺 FL/TX/NC
  • Merchant Opportunity: Chicago restaurant/retail owners opening FL/TX locations
  • Strategy: Multi-location merchant accounts (IL + FL/TX)

Sun Belt States (FL, TX, NC, SC, GA, AZ)

North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Florida, Texas, Arizona are TOP GAINERS in domestic migration. International migration also surging (NC: +47K, AZ shifting to domestic).

🔺 Highest Inbound Migration
  • Merchant Opportunity: New businesses opening daily to serve growing populations
  • Strategy: First-mover advantage in NC/SC/GA before Square/Clover saturate

How Each Payment Method Works

📱

QR Code Payments

Best for: Retail counters, food trucks, pop-up vendors

Merchant Flow
  1. Open Payphone Business app
  2. Tap "Receive Payment"
  3. Enter amount (e.g., $24.50)
  4. Tap "Generate QR Code"
  5. Show QR on screen or print for signage
  6. Customer scans with any phone camera
  7. Payment arrives in Payphone wallet instantly
Customer Flow
  1. Open phone camera or Payphone app
  2. Scan merchant's QR code
  3. Review payment details ($24.50 to "Taquería El Sabor")
  4. Select payment method (Payphone wallet, card, bank)
  5. Confirm payment with Face ID/fingerprint
  6. Receive instant digital receipt
  7. Merchant gets paid — transaction complete
Stripe Infrastructure: Stripe Payment Intents API generates unique QR codes tied to payment sessions. Customer pays via Stripe Checkout (hosted) or Payphone wallet direct debit.
💳

Tap to Pay (NFC)

Best for: In-person payments, mobile services, home contractors

Merchant Flow
  1. Open Payphone Business app
  2. Tap "Tap to Pay" mode
  3. Enter amount (e.g., $150.00 for plumbing service)
  4. Tap "Ready to Accept Card"
  5. Customer taps their credit/debit card on phone
  6. Phone vibrates + shows "Payment Approved ✓"
  7. Digital receipt sent to customer via SMS/email
Customer Flow
  1. Pull out credit/debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
  2. Tap card on merchant's phone (NFC antenna area)
  3. Wait 1-2 seconds for approval beep
  4. Payment complete — no signature needed (<$100)
  5. Receive SMS receipt with merchant name and details
  6. Transaction appears on card statement instantly
  7. No app download needed for customer
Stripe Infrastructure: Stripe Terminal SDK (Tap to Pay on iPhone/Android) converts merchant's phone into PCI-compliant NFC terminal. No hardware reader required. Works with all contactless cards and wallets.
Device Compatibility: iPhone XS and newer (iOS 15.4+), Android phones with NFC (Android 10+). Covers 95%+ of US smartphones.
🔗

Link to Pay

Best for: Remote invoices, professional services, recurring billing

Merchant Flow
  1. Open Payphone Business app
  2. Tap "Create Payment Link"
  3. Enter amount + description ("Tax prep service - Maria Rodriguez")
  4. Add customer's phone/email (optional)
  5. Tap "Generate Link"
  6. Share via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or social media
  7. Track link status (sent, viewed, paid) in app
  8. Get notified when customer pays
Customer Flow
  1. Receive payment link via text, WhatsApp, email
  2. Tap link to open secure Payphone payment page
  3. Review merchant name, amount, description
  4. Enter card details (or use saved payment method)
  5. Tap "Pay $500.00" to confirm
  6. Receive instant receipt and confirmation
  7. Payment link expires after use (single-use security)
