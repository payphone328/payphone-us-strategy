07 — Phase 3: Product Expansion
What Cash App built for 58M users, we’re building for the community they forgot. Eight products. All Spanish-first. All cross-border.
Tandas
Ahorro en comunidad
Rotating savings circles — digitized. 6-12 members, monthly contributions, automated payouts. The cultural tradition Cash App tried with “Pools” but without the trust.
Inspired by: Cash App Pools (1.7M users)
Payphone Score
Tu historial es tu crédito
Alternative credit scoring from remittance history, bill payments, and wallet activity. 70% of our target users have FICO below 580 — we build credit from what they already do.
Inspired by: Cash App Score (38% more approvals)
Paga Después (BNPL)
Compra ahora, paga después
Buy now, pay later for remittance-linked purchases. Split payments into 4 installments. Enable family in LATAM to receive goods, not just cash.
Inspired by: Cash App Afterpay ($2B lending run rate)
Cobra Antes (Early Pay)
Tu sueldo, 2 días antes
Get paid up to 2 days early with direct deposit. Free overdraft protection up to $200 for active users. Target: Hispanic workforce with weekly/biweekly pay cycles.
Inspired by: Cash App Direct Deposit (58M users)
Payphone Familia
Cuentas familiares sin fronteras
Parent in the US, child in Ecuador. Shared visibility, controlled spending, real-time notifications across borders. No competitor spans both sides.
Inspired by: Cash App Families (US-only, ages 13-17)
Boosts
Descuentos instantáneos
Instant cashback at Hispanic-serving merchants — bodegas, taquerías, tiendas. Activate a Boost before you pay. 5-15% back, driving spend to Payphone Card.
Inspired by: Cash App Boosts (free, no cap)
Tax + ITIN Filing
Declara gratis. Obtén tu ITIN.
Free federal + state tax filing in Spanish. ITIN application assistance built in. Convert undocumented users into ITIN holders → deeper financial access.
Inspired by: Cash App Taxes (100% free)
Ahorra (Savings)
Tu dinero trabaja para ti
High-yield savings on wallet balance via Stripe Treasury. FDIC-insured. Earn up to 4% APY. Auto-save rules: round-up purchases, percentage of direct deposit.
Inspired by: Cash App Green (3.75% APY)
COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGE
Cash App has 58M users and 24% Hispanic adoption — but treats them as a byproduct, not the mission.
Every product above is Cash App-inspired but purpose-built for immigrants. Spanish-first. Cross-border native. ITIN-compatible. Cash App requires SSN. Cash App has zero remittances. Cash App has zero merchant acquiring for small businesses. We take their playbook and build it for the 50M they ignore.
Phase 3 Timeline
Products ship in priority order after Phase 1 (cards) and Phase 2 (merchant acquiring) are live. Early Pay + Savings = Q1 2027 (Stripe Treasury). Tandas + Boosts = Q2 2027 (community features). BNPL + Payphone Score = Q3 2027 (lending). Tax Filing + Familia = Q4 2027 (ecosystem completion).

LATAM Local Payment Methods (Stripe)
Critical for unbanked populations. Cash-to-digital bridges that no US neobank offers in-market.
OXXO
Cash Payments
Pay with cash at 20,000+ OXXO stores across Mexico. Customer gets a voucher, pays at counter. Critical for unbanked. Mexico only.
SPEI
Instant Bank Transfer
Mexico’s real-time interbank transfer system. Free, instant, used by 90M+ accounts. The backbone of Mexican digital payments. Mexico only.
MSI
Meses Sin Intereses
Interest-free installments (3, 6, 9, 12 months). Massively popular in Mexico. Drives 30-40% higher AOV. Native BNPL before BNPL existed. Mexico only.
Boleto
Boleto Bancário
Brazil’s cash payment voucher. Print & pay at any bank branch, ATM, or lottery house. 70M+ Brazilians use it. Future: if Brazil enters pipeline.
* All methods require local Stripe entity. OXXO + SPEI + MSI are critical for Mexico Tier 1 launch (Q3 2026). These are not optional — they are table stakes for the Mexican market.
