Colombia Betting Expansion Playbook

Phase 1: API Integration

Offer the API (tokenization + web transaction + payouts) to 2–3 Coljuegos-licensed operators. The API is the most betting-ready product today. Lead with card deposits + Pagos Masivos for payouts.

Phase 2: Add PSE + Wallets

Integrate PSE for bank transfer deposits and Nequi/Daviplata as deposit methods. This captures the 55% of banked Colombians who prefer non-card payments for betting.

Phase 3: Cash Deposits via Efecty

Enable cash-in at 12K+ Efecty locations. This is the key to reaching the 40% cash-dependent population. Generate voucher in betting app → pay at Efecty → funds credited instantly.

Phase 4: Payphone as Betting Wallet

Position the Payphone wallet + Mastercard as the player's betting account. Deposit from any source, winnings go to wallet, spend via card. Becomes a closed-loop ecosystem.

Coljuegos Advantage

Colombia's Coljuegos license is ~$120K — cheapest in LATAM. No IEPS-equivalent tax on gaming revenue (unlike Mexico's 30%). This makes Colombia the highest-margin betting market in the region.

Card Gap → Opportunity

The Payphone Mastercard can't do ATM withdrawals today. For betting, this is actually a feature: winnings stay in the Payphone ecosystem. But add Nequi/Daviplata withdrawal as an alternative cash-out path.

Colombia Strategic Takeaways

🚀 Fast to Market

Lower upfront investment ($570K) vs Mexico ($896K). Cheaper Coljuegos license ($120K) makes betting viable from day one.

💳 Wallet Ecosystem

Nequi + Daviplata = 32M combined users. This is a major differentiator that doesn't exist at this scale in most other LATAM markets.

⚠️ GMF Tax Drag

The 0.4% financial movement tax is unavoidable and eats into margins on every bank transfer. Build this into pricing from day one.

💸 FX Opportunity

COP volatility creates wider FX spreads. The +0.9% FX margin is higher than Mexico's +0.8% due to currency risk premium.

🎯 Betting is Key

At ~3.1% net margin, the betting vertical is 2x more profitable than standard card processing. Coljuegos license should be a Year 1 priority.

📈 Path to Scale

Launch cross-border via dLocal, migrate to Redeban/Credibanco as volume grows. Target 5,000 merchants by Year 3 for $451M TPV.
