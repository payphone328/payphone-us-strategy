Decision 1: LATAM BD Hire

Internal vs. External vs. Contractor

Recommended: External hire. This role requires deep LATAM fintech relationships, regulatory navigation skills, and full-time dedication. A contractor lacks commitment; internal reallocation would weaken other workstreams.

Deadline: End of April 2026. Delay past this date puts Q3 Mexico and Colombia targets at risk.

Decision 2: Ecuador Priority

Tier 1 (home market) vs. Tier 2 (reg risk)

Recommended: Tier 2. Ecuador’s first fintech regulation (Sept 2024) is too new. Regulatory requirements are still being defined. $200K capital requirement adds cost. Payphone already operates there — no urgency to add card issuance immediately.

Deadline: Now. This is a sequencing decision that affects resource allocation across all tiers.

Decision 3: Panama Betting

Keep exploratory vs. Activate

Recommended: Activate. $40K initial license cost is low. Panama uses USD (zero FX risk). iGaming market grew +53% in 2025. Market projected to reach $4.9B by 2032. PayCaddy already based there — leverage existing infrastructure.

Deadline: Now. World Cup 2026 window makes timing critical for activation.
